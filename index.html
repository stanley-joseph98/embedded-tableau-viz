Access to population control services in Sub-Saharan Africa is a big challenge. These challenges are mainly attributed to poverty, lack of or inadequate education, climatic conditions and government incapabilities. The International Planned Parenthood Federation as an NGO with keen interest in the human rights has been on the forefront in ensuring that these services are availed to those in need. To excel in this, IPPF employs a wide range of mechanisms to ensure its clients are have access to population management services that they need. One of such mechanisms implemented is data visualization, which aimms to streamline the process of understanding Africa's demographic. This web page presents a summary of visualizations that have aided in population management in Zambia
